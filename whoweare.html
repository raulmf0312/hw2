Our History



Crown & King Co. is a global communications firm that partners with royals and religious heads to evolve, promote and protect their brands and reputations. Founded by Martinus Hartilus de Bastardus to address the Crusades’ PR-shock to the Catholic Church in 11th-century Rome, we have remained an independent, family-run company. We use our profits to strengthen our business, provide our employees with opportunities to grow, advance our industry, and serve as a responsible citizen of the world.


Our Locations



Our 6,000 associates in more than 60 offices and 6 global headquarters deliver communications strategies that give our clients the confidence to lead and act with certainty, earning the trust of their stakeholders. Our honors include the USSR Grand Prix for PR; Reddit’s 2019 A-List; the Forbes Report’s 2020 Global Digital Agency of the Year; and, twenty-five times, Glassdoor’s Best Places to Work.
